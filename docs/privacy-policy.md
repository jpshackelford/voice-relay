# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 18, 2026**

Voice Relay ("Voice Relay", "we," or "us") provides real-time voice and text communication services through our website located at https://vr.chorecraft.net/ (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information when you use our Service.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you authenticate with our Service, we collect:

- **OAuth Profile Information:** Your name, email address, and profile picture from GitHub or Google OAuth
- **User Identifier:** A unique identifier from your OAuth provider to link your account

We do not collect or store your OAuth passwords. Authentication is handled entirely by GitHub and Google.

### Content You Provide

When you use the Service, we may collect:

- **Voice Recordings:** Audio captured during voice relay sessions (processed temporarily for speech-to-text conversion)
- **Text Messages:** Messages sent through the relay system
- **Conversation History:** Messages within workspace sessions for display purposes

### Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect:

- **Device Information:** Browser type, device type, and operating system
- **Usage Data:** Pages visited, features used, and session duration
- **Technical Data:** IP address and general location (city/region level)

### Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage to:

- Remember your device preferences
- Store session tokens for authentication
- Maintain workspace and device settings

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide the Service:** Relay voice and text messages between your devices
- **Authenticate Users:** Verify your identity through OAuth providers
- **Improve the Service:** Understand usage patterns to enhance features
- **Communicate:** Send service-related notices when necessary

### Voice Data Processing

Voice recordings are processed temporarily for speech-to-text conversion and are not permanently stored on our servers. Transcribed text may be retained as part of session conversation history.

### AI Assistant Integration

If you use the AI assistant feature (OpenHands integration):

- Your messages may be sent to the OpenHands API for AI processing
- Responses from OpenHands are displayed in your session
- Refer to the [OpenHands Privacy Policy](https://www.all-hands.dev/privacy) for how they handle data

### Text-to-Speech (Optional)

If you enable ElevenLabs text-to-speech:

- Text content is sent to ElevenLabs for voice synthesis
- Refer to the [ElevenLabs Privacy Policy](https://elevenlabs.io/privacy-policy) for their data practices

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with:

### Service Providers

Third-party services that help us operate the Service:

- **GitHub OAuth:** Authentication ([GitHub Privacy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement))
- **Google OAuth:** Authentication ([Google Privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
- **OpenHands API:** AI assistant features ([OpenHands Privacy](https://www.all-hands.dev/privacy))
- **ElevenLabs:** Text-to-speech (optional, user-enabled) ([ElevenLabs Privacy](https://elevenlabs.io/privacy-policy))

### Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or if we believe disclosure is necessary to:

- Comply with legal processes
- Protect our rights or property
- Ensure the safety of users

## Data Retention

- **Account Information:** Retained while your account is active
- **Session Content:** Conversation history is retained for your workspace sessions
- **Voice Recordings:** Processed temporarily and not permanently stored
- **Usage Data:** Retained for analytics purposes

You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

## Your Choices

### Account Management

You can:

- Review and update your profile information
- Delete your workspaces and associated data
- Sign out to revoke session access

### OAuth Permissions

You can revoke Voice Relay's access to your account through:

- [GitHub Applications Settings](https://github.com/settings/applications)
- [Google Account Permissions](https://myaccount.google.com/permissions)

### Browser Controls

You can:

- Clear local storage and cookies through your browser settings
- Use browser privacy modes to limit data collection

## Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information, including:

- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Secure token-based authentication
- Database access controls

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us.

## International Users

Voice Relay is operated from the United States. If you access the Service from outside the United States, your information may be transferred to and processed in the United States.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
